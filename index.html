Gibraltar Gaels Masters 2026 Fundraiser

How It Works

1. Choose one golfer from each of the five groups below.

2. Your golfers score points based on where they finish within their group:

  • 1st in group: 5 points
  • 2nd in group: 4 points
  • 3rd in group: 3 points
  • 4th in group: 2 points
  • 5th in group: 1 point
  • Missed cut: -1 point

3. Bonus points are available for:

  • +5 points if your golfer wins the Masters
  • +2 points if your golfer is the tournament leader after Round 1

4. If players tie for a position within a group, dead-heat scoring applies. The points for the tied positions are added together and divided equally between those tied players.

Example: if 3 players tie for 2nd place, the points for 2nd, 3rd and 4th are added together (4 + 3 + 2 = 9), so each player receives 3 points.

5. Entry is €10. Winner takes all, with tied winners splitting the prize pool equally.

6. To enter, send your payment by Revolut and then submit your picks using the form below.
